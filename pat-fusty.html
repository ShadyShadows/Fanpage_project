Pat Fusty

the Giant Monkey

"Pat is a huge monkey of enormous strength. His unique size and power is a great asset in the war on Bloons."

Pat has a very small range but does massive damage and can hit all bloon types exept camo. He also can use rallying roar to increase damage of all towers in his range. he also can slap bloons forcing them back to the start of the track.
