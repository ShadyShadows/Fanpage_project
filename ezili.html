Ezili

the Voodoo Monkey

"Ezili is a wielder of dark arts and manipulator of Bloons. Beware."

Ezili places hexs on bloons dealing damage over time. She can pop leads and camos, but not purple bloons. She struggles with swarms of bloons but is excellent at popping moabs and buffs necromancer monkeys. She also at levle 20 can kill a BAD all by herself, no other hero can do that.
