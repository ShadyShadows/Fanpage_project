Adora

the High Priestess

"Adora's devotion compels her to strike Bloons down with furious vengeance."

Adora has high pierce homing attacks that ignore cover, pop leads, but can not pop purple bloons. She is extremely good at dealing with hordes of bloons. Like summoning sun gods she can sacrifice monkeys to buff her range, attack speed, rate of fire, and give her huge amounts of xp. She can also be buffed by summoning the vengefull sun god.
